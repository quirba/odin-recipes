Flax Bun Burgers

Description

See how to make homemade burgers that are hearty, flavorful and full of vegetables

Ingredients

  • 300g Minced Beef
  • 2 thin slices Tasty Cheese
  • 1 cup Baby Spinach
  • 2 tablespoons Garlic Aioli

Bun

  • 4 tablespoons Butter (Melted)
  • 1 pinch Salt & Pepper To Taste
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 8 tablespoons Flaxseed Meal
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Mix together butter, salt, eggs, flaxseed meal and baking powder in a microwave safe bowl or container.
  2. Put the bowl / container in the microwave and nuke for 1 minute or until center is set. It will billow up in the bowl
  3. Leave it in the bowl for another minute then turn it out on a cooling rack and let it cool
  4. Once it has cooled take the long bread knife and slice the bun in half
  5. Create beef patties with the mince. Heat frying pan with teaspoon of olive oil and cook to your liking
  6. Place cooked patties onto the bun. Add cheese, aioli, and spinach
